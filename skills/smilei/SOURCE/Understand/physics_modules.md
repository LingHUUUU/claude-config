# Physics modules[¶](#physics-modules "Link to this heading")

The basic [PIC algorithms](algorithms.html) do not reproduce all the physics of a plasma.
For instance, the typical cell size is too coarse to model [collisions](collisions.html)
so that a specific module is necessary. Similarly, atomic physics and
quantum processes require physics modules such as [Ionization](ionization.html) and
[High-energy photon emission & radiation reaction](radiation_loss.html).

- [Binary collisions & reactions](collisions.html)
- [Ionization](ionization.html)
- [High-energy photon emission & radiation reaction](radiation_loss.html)
- [Multiphoton Breit-Wheeler pair creation](multiphoton_Breit_Wheeler.html)
