# Parallelization & optimization[¶](#parallelization-optimization "Link to this heading")

To reach high performances on supercomputers, several techniques have
been developed in Smilei.
While a good understanding of [parallelization](parallelization.html) is mandatory
to run a meaningful PIC simulation for any supercomputer user, other advanced
techniques, such as [Vectorization](vectorization.html), are optional even though they can
provide important advantages.
